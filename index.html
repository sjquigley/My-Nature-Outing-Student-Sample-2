Language the experience of nature

Beech Tree, Cherry Tree, fungi, decay, Black Eyed Susan, Black Berries, Cicadas, Chipmunks, Acorns.

On the nature walk, we first went into the forest. There were lots of different animals including cicadas and chipmunks that lived in the lush beech trees that towered over us. We had to climb over decaying logs that had fungi growing on them. We then crossed a little creek that lead us to the majestic meadows that was blooming with flowers and little berries. The dirt path lead us back to the Frick Enviormental Center.
